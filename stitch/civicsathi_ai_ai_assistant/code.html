Applying for a new Ration Card in Maharashtra can be done online through the Aaple Sarkar portal or offline at the local Food and Civil Supplies office. The process involves identity verification, address proof, and income declaration.

folder_open Required Documents

  • check Identity Proof (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, or PAN)
  • check Address Proof (Electricity Bill, Rent Agreement)
  • check Income Certificate (Issued by Tahsildar)
  • check Passport size photographs of the family head
